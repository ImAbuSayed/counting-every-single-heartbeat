[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the fire when your shadow falls on me
A quiet spark beneath the canopy of trees
You pull me closer in the fading evening light
We’re making memories that echo through the night

[Verse 1]
The embers settle on the edges of the fire
We’re wrapped together in a blanket of desire
I trace the lines upon the back of your soft hand
And read the future that the universe has planned

[Pre-Chorus]
The breeze is whispering a secret to the pines
While every movement leaves the noisy world behind
I pull my jacket round your shoulders in the cold
And let the magic of the midnight hour unfold

[Chorus]
I’m counting every single heartbeat in the dark
A steady rhythm like a drum inside your chest
I’m counting every single heartbeat in the dark
There is no other place I ever want to rest

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, just the rhythm of our souls
Losing all of our control

[Verse 2]
Your gaze is catching on the shadows in the brush
A quiet longing underneath the twilight hush
We trade a smile without a single word to speak
A kind of language that the restless never seek

[Pre-Chorus 2]
The tension builds with every breath we slowly take
A sweet surrender that is beautiful to make
I pull my jacket round your shoulders in the cold
And let the magic of the midnight hour unfold

[Chorus]
I’m counting every single heartbeat in the dark
A steady rhythm like a drum inside your chest
I’m counting every single heartbeat in the dark
There is no other place I ever want to rest

[Post-Chorus 2]
Oh, just the rhythm of our souls
Losing all of our control

[Bridge]
The temperature is rising underneath the moon
Our heavy breathing is the only honest tune
I want to memorize the heat upon your skin
And find the heaven where the earthly things begin
You got Saeed completely tangled in your spell
A deep desire that my lips could never tell
